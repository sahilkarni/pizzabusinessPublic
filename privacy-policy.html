Privacy Policy

Your data and our WhatsApp bot.

This Privacy Policy explains how Pizza Business collects, uses, and protects personal information when you contact us or place orders through our WhatsApp bot.

Effective date: March 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use our WhatsApp bot, we may collect:

  • Your name and phone number.
  • Your messages, order details, and delivery or pickup preferences.
  • Any information you voluntarily share during customer support.

2. How We Use Information

  • To respond to messages and process pizza orders.
  • To confirm, update, or complete deliveries or pickups.
  • To provide customer support and improve service quality.
  • To maintain security and prevent misuse of the bot.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only when needed to operate our business, such as with payment, delivery, hosting, or messaging service providers, or when required by law.

4. Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill orders, provide support, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your information where applicable. You can also stop using the bot at any time by ending the WhatsApp conversation.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

Our service may rely on third-party platforms such as WhatsApp and Meta. Your use of those platforms is also subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

8. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, please contact Pizza Business through the same WhatsApp business number you use to place orders.
